Chef Tips

Tips and tricks from our in-house chefs c;

7 / 3 / 20

Olive Oil

Don’t cook with extra-virgin olive oil, by doing so it looses all it’s complex flavours. Save your extra-virgin olive oil for salads, garnish or other uncooked applications, and use regular cheap olive oil for cooking.


29 / 2 / 20

Salt

Salt as you go! Never be afraid of salt; using fresh ingredients means a much lower sodium content to begin with compared to packaged foods.


22 / 2 / 20

Mise en place

Have everything pre-chopped/peeled/measured/etc before you even start cooking, so that you're not looking for that white wine while your shallots are burning.
